About Us

Noble Dynamic is a data and AI consultancy with over 23 years of experience in software engineering, data science, and technical strategy.

With 15 years of hands-on experience in AI, computer vision, and imaging, we bring a depth of knowledge that goes well beyond the current generation of tools. We know what works in practice, not just what looks good in a demo.

Our founder built and led Zegami, a data visualisation and exploration platform that spun out of the University of Oxford, serving as CTO and CEO for seven years. Zegami is a continually evolving platform, and we regularly integrate it into client projects where visual exploration of complex data can unlock insights that traditional dashboards miss.

That mix of hands-on technical work and experience running a product company means we bring a perspective most consultancies can't. We understand product, strategy, and commercial reality, not just the engineering.

We're at our best on projects that push boundaries: prototyping novel ideas, applying techniques across domains, or helping teams figure out what's technically possible and how to get there.
